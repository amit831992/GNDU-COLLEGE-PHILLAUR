O.S.D. Message

Prof. (Dr.) Parmjit Kaur Jassal

Dear Students,

Guru Nanak Dev University College, Phillaur fulfills the need of higher education in the rural area of district Jalandhar. It is a matter of great pleasure for me that I have been awarded opportunity to serve this rural institution by Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar.

It has been an excellent experience to be in personal contact with the students during working days till covid-19 disrupted this in 2020. Classes were conducted online and examination system has come to be a mode set by the University.

Before the second wave, exams were conducted offline but the tsunami of covid cases compelled us to teach online. Teachers have apprised me about your difficulties such as, lacking smart phone, mobile data and network.

Students, you are lucky to have a very dedicated group of teachers for all courses. If you have good intension of learning the above-mentioned difficulties can easily be overcome. Take maximum help from teachers and your fellow class mates. Learning is much more important that just getting degrees. Knowledge is power, try to attain it.

This difficult time shall too pass. Life will be normal and we shall regularly meet in the campus. Stay safe and take care of your health.

Stay blessed.
