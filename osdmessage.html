O.S.D. Message

Prof. (Dr.) Parmjit Kaur Jassal

Dear Students, The world is passing through an unforeseen and ever toughest phase in the history of human health. The whole world has been stalled due to this Public Health Emergency of International Concern that has not only disrupted the normal routine life but has also created a lot of uncertainty. Your regular studies and learning have been suffering since the beginning of lockdown in the country. Your teachers tried their best to reach you online to prepare you for the even (2nd, 4th, 6th) semester examination which again till now is uncertain to be held. Now the procedure of admission to the next semester or to the new course is to begin. We have prepared a brochure for your information as to how you can register yourself online. You are young, energetic and full of dreams of a successful life. We are committed to help you in achieving your goals so that you do not waste a precious year of your life. You will be getting lectures and notes from your teachers online until the environment is conducive for starting the regular campus studies.My best wishes to all of you for a beautiful life ahead. Hope to get a healthy response from all of you during these tough times.
